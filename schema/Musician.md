# Musician Schema

Schema Version: v0.1

## Purpose

Represents musicians and other individuals contributing to jazz culture.

## Permanent ID

MUS-000001

## Required Fields

- Musician ID
- Full Name
- Native Name
- Alternate Name(s)
- Birth Date
- Death Date
- Country
- Primary Instrument
- Additional Instruments
- Primary Roles
- Active Years
- Official Website
- Notes

## Primary Roles

- Performer
- Composer
- Arranger
- Conductor
- Educator
- Producer
- Researcher
- Critic
- Photographer
- Other

## Relationships

A Musician may perform at many Events.

A Musician may belong to multiple Organizations.

A Musician may participate in multiple Units.

A Musician may have mentor and student relationships.

A Musician may collaborate with many other Musicians.
